The Pawn

The pawn is the most numerous piece in chess, typically starting in the second row. It moves forward one square at a time, but captures diagonally. On its first move, a pawn can move two squares forward. If it reaches the opposite end of the board, it can be promoted to another piece (except the king).

The Rook

The rook is a powerful piece that moves vertically or horizontally across the board. It is used for both offense and defense, and it can be used to control open files and ranks.
